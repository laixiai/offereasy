Becoming a true Performance Expert goes beyond technical campaign management; it's about transforming into a strategic partner for the client. The core of this role lies in the ability to listen, understand, and translate a client's business challenges into a tangible advertising strategy that delivers measurable results. *   **Potential Follow-up Questions**ï¼š

### Question 6ï¼šHow would you explain the value of a full-funnel video strategy to a client who is only focused on direct-response, bottom-funnel metrics?
*   **Points of Assessment**ï¼šThis question tests your ability to educate clients and think strategically about long-term brand building versus short-term performance.
*   **Standard Answer**ï¼š"I would acknowledge the importance of their direct-response goals and show how a full-funnel strategy actually supports and enhances them. ### Question 7ï¼šWalk me through how you would use data and analytics to optimize a video campaign mid-flight.
*   **Points of Assessment**ï¼šAssesses your data literacy, your familiarity with key advertising metrics, and your ability to make data-driven decisions to improve campaign performance.
*   **Standard Answer**ï¼š"Mid-flight optimization starts with a regular review of key performance indicators against the campaign's primary goal. *   **Common Pitfalls**ï¼š

*   **Potential Follow-up Questions**ï¼š

### Question 9ï¼šWhat, in your opinion, are the key components of a highly effective video ad creative?
*   **Points of Assessment**ï¼šTests your understanding of creative best practices in the context of online video, where user attention is scarce.
*   **Standard Answer**ï¼š"A highly effective video ad grabs attention within the first 3-5 seconds with a strong hook. The message should be clear and focused on a single call-to-action that tells the viewer exactly what to do next. Finally, it needs to be tailored to the platform and audience; a 6-second bumper ad has very different creative needs than a 2-minute TrueView in-stream ad. Testing different creative variations is also a key component to find what resonates best."
*   **Common Pitfalls**ï¼š
    *   Giving generic advice that could apply to any form of advertising (e.g., "it should be engaging").
    *   Forgetting platform-specific nuances like mobile-first viewing or the importance of the initial few seconds.
*   **Potential Follow-up Questions**ï¼š
    *   How would you advise a client with a limited budget on creating effective video ads?
    *   Can you give an example of a brand you think does video advertising well, and why?
    *   How do you use data to inform creative strategy?
